What does a counselling psychologist do? +

A counseling psychologist fosters well-being by helping people with their emotional, social, work, school, and physical health concerns, using a variety of therapeutic techniques.

Therapy

Offers psychological support to improve mental well-being.

Assessments

Evaluates emotional and behavioral issues.

Guidance

Helps clients cope with stress, trauma, and life challenges.

Common Mental Health Myths Busted +

Myth: Mental health problems are rare.

Fact: They affect millions worldwide and can impact anyone, regardless of age or background.

Myth: Therapy is only for serious problems.

Fact: Therapy is for anyone looking to improve their mental well-being and develop better coping skills.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) +

What can I expect in my first session?

It's an opportunity for us to discuss your goals and concerns, setting the foundation for our work together.

Is counselling confidential?

Yes, everything discussed is confidential, with exceptions only for risk of harm to yourself or others.

How long does each session last?

Typically, sessions last between 45-60 minutes, depending on individual needs.
